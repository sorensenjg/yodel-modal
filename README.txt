Yodel WP brings you a unique way to reach your audience with custom popups.

== Description ==

Inspired by the ancient yodels of the Central Alps, Yodel brings you a unique way to reach your audience with custom popups. Just as yodelers once communicated across mountains, this plugin lets you share your message effectively and engagingly.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `yodel-wp.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
